OUR MISSION
To inspire students have passion for coding, problem solving, building the future and aspire future generations.
OUR VISION
Develop community that is be able to provide effective, efficient and creative students for the future.
OUR GOAL
Aims to help students become competent and confident users who can use the basic knowledge and skills acquired to assist them in their daily lives. It is also supposed to prepare students for the world of tomorrow. It also aims to help learners to have an open and flexible mind.
